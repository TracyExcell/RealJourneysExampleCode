Te Anau Caves Wharf Resource Consent Number #203806
Parties Environment Southland & Real Journey Ltd.
Term 19 years
Expiry date: 1st August 2025
Fees: Admin Charge: $45.00 P/A
Activity To remove, alter, extend, erect and use a wharf over and in a lake and to deposit concrete in a lake
Location Lake Te Anau foreshore at Te Anau Caves

Conditions:

  • The wharf is to be kept in good condition
  • No further alteration is permitted
  • The wharf is to be inspected every 5 years by a qualified person and a report is to be sent to the council before the 31st of December of that year
  • The Real Journeys Limited is liable for injury that occurs on the wharf due to our neglect
  • Upon expiry of this consent the consent holder is to remove the entire structure in full, then restore the site to its original condition.
  • In the discovery, or suspected discovery of a site of cultural importance the consent holder shall immediately cease operations
  • All rubbish is to be removed
  • In the event of contamination Real Journeys Limited shall immediately clean up the site and inform Environment Southland
  • Any authorised officer from Environment Southland may enter the wharf and inspect the structure. If repair is needed Real Journeys Limited must complete this within a reasonable time. Failure to do so can result in the loss of the consent
  • The consent can be reviewed annually by Environment Southland
